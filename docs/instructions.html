Section 04

Protocol Stacks

Group related items into named stacks for organized protocol management.

What Are Stacks?

A protocol stack is a named collection of supplements, medications, therapies, and peptides that work together toward a specific goal.

📚 Sleep Stack

Magnesium Glycinate + Ashwagandha + Muse Meditation + Box Breathing

Active

💪 Performance Stack

Creatine + BPC-157 + Zone 2 Cardio + Compression Boots

Active

✨ Skin Protocol

Retinoid + LED Face Mask + Gua Sha + Microneedling + Infrared Panel

Scheduled

💇 Hair Protocol

Finasteride + Minoxidil + Dermaroller Scalp + LED Hair Cap

Active
✓ Tip: Items can belong to multiple stacks. Your "Morning Fasted" supplement might appear in both your Sleep Stack and your Performance Stack.

Stack Activation

Stacks can be active (items show in your daily plan), scheduled (will activate on a future date), or inactive. Toggle stack status to quickly enable or disable entire protocols without losing your configuration.

What's in a Supplement Profile?

SectionWhat It Tells You
MechanismHow the substance works in your body at a biochemical level.
DosagesTypical dose ranges for different goals (maintenance, deficiency, performance).
TimingBest time windows, whether to take with food, and what to avoid.
InteractionsKnown interactions with medications and other supplements with severity levels.
SynergiesBeneficial pairings and why they work better together.
CyclingWhether periodic breaks are recommended and suggested patterns.
SafetyContraindications, side effects, and safety considerations.

Section 09

Adherence Tracking

Monitor your consistency and build healthy habits with built-in adherence tracking.

How Tracking Works

When you check off an item in your daily plan, an adherence event is recorded with a timestamp. The tracking summary card on Today shows your rolling 14-day adherence rate.

📊 Tap the tracking summary card to see a detailed breakdown of adherence by item, category, and time window.

Cycle Alerts

For supplements with cycling recommendations (like Ashwagandha's 8-on/2-off pattern), the app tracks your cycle position and alerts you when it's time to take a break or resume.
